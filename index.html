Welcome to our English project, a digital journey where each section tells the unique story of a student — their past, present, and future — through the lens of creativity and technology.

About this project

This site is part of an exposition titled "Coding my past, designing my future". Here, we explore how our experiences have shaped who we are today and how we aim to design our tomorrow. Each page reflects a student’s memories, turning points, hobbies, and dreams — all crafted with code.

Explore our stories
